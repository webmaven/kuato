About Kuato

Kuato is a powerful tool that gives you unprecedented control over your browsing experience. Inspired by the iconic character, Kuato helps you see what's really there.

Features

  • Import from URL: Load any article, story, or book from a URL for processing.
  • Automatic Text Chunking: Automatically splits long texts into smaller, manageable chunks, perfect for AI conversations.
  • Sequential Sending: Send text chunks one by one ("Send Next") or all at once ("Send All"), waiting for your AI to respond between each part.
  • Local Library: All your loaded texts are saved in a local library on your browser for easy access.
  • Simple Management: Easily select, rename, or delete books from your library.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2025-09-20

Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains what data the Kuato extension collects and how it is used.

1. Data Collection and Storage

Kuato is designed to be privacy-first. All of your data, including the books you load and your personal settings, is stored exclusively on your local machine using the browser's built-in storage. This data never leaves your computer and is never transmitted to the developer or any third-party servers.

2. Permissions

The Kuato extension requests two main permissions:

  • Access to all URLs (<all_urls>): This permission is required for the core functionality of the extension, which is to fetch the content of a URL (like an article or an e-book) that you provide. The extension only ever fetches URLs that you explicitly ask it to. It does not access your browsing history or data from any other website automatically.
  • Access to fars.ee: This permission is used for the "Send" functionality, as explained below.

3. Use of Third-Party Services (Pastebin)

To send text to your AI companion, Kuato breaks the text into smaller chunks. To avoid issues with message length and formatting, each chunk is uploaded to a public, anonymous pastebin service (fars.ee). This generates a unique URL for that chunk of text, which is then sent to the AI.

This process is anonymous:

  • Only the text chunk itself is uploaded.
  • No information about you, your account, or your browsing activity is included in this upload.
  • The resulting pastebin URL is not associated with you.

4. No Analytics or Tracking

The Kuato extension does not include any analytics, tracking codes, or advertising. The developer does not and cannot track your usage of the extension.

5. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
